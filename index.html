Book Rooms with ease

BookIt is the software used internally by EnergySavvy so we can instantly book rooms for impromptu meetings.

Why?

We at EnergySavvy love the Google Calendar platform for managing shared, schedulable resources, but when we needed a room right now the Google Calendar interface was too clunky. Rooms were poached, room squatters were rampant, and anarchy reigned. Nobody wants to get kicked out of a room or show up for their rightfully-booked meeting only to find the room occupied. Our Seattleite non-confrontational attitude forced us to find a solution.

Open Source

BookIt was built at EnergySavvy as a hackathon project because the off-the-shelf options were expensive and clunky. We've open-sourced it so that you can use it yourself. Just create your own .json data file with your resource IDs and floor layout. Instructions are in our README.
